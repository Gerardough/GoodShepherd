The versatile purple yam that one cannot resist

The mission of this website is to provide an in-depth knowledge of Good Shepherd's Ube Jam.

Good Shepherd's Ube Jam is a beloved delicacy in the Philippines, particularly known for its high quality and rich flavor. Made from purple yam (ube), this jam is a signature product of the Good Shepherd Convent in Baguio City.

Key Feature:

DYK? The jam has a vibrant purple hue, which is naturally derived from the ube itself, a root crop native to the Philippines. he taste is a perfect balance of sweetness and the earthy flavor of ube, making it unique compared to other sweet spreads. The jam is traditionally handmade by nuns and volunteers at the Good Shepherd Convent.

Culinary Uses:

DYK? Good Shepherd's Ube Jam is versatile and can be used in various ways: Spread on bread, crackers, or pastries, as a topping for halo-halo, a popular Filipino dessert, or mixed into ice cream, cakes, and other desserts. It's a must-try for those visiting Baguio and a favorite pasalubong (souvenir).
